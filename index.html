Services

Recording

Mixing

Based on songs of up to 48 tracks and 5 minutes in length.

Included in mix:

  • 1 song mixed and mastered
  • TV Mix (mix without lead vocal)
  • PA Mix (instrumental)
  • 2 revisions per song

If you’d prefer to master the song elsewhere, I will provide you with an unmastered version as well.

Stems available at extra cost.

Mastering

Included in price:

  • 1 song mastered
  • 44.1k/16bit master (main format for streaming platforms and CDs)
  • High-quality master (in original format)
  • 2 revisions

Additional cost for added formats (radio edit, instrumentals etc).

DDP Master available for albums/EPs at extra cost.
